üÜò Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Q: "Transaction failed" error?

  • Check you have enough Sepolia ETH for gas
  • Verify you have enough VLFY balance
  • Make sure you're on Sepolia network
  • Try increasing gas limit in MetaMask

Q: Can't claim faucet?

  • Must wait 24 hours between claims
  • Check cooldown timer on dashboard
  • Ensure wallet is connected

Q: Subscription not showing as active?

  • Click "Refresh Status" button
  • Wait 30-60 seconds for blockchain confirmation
  • Check transaction on Etherscan
