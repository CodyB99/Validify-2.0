Discord: The #1 Attack Vector for Web3

Web3 communities have lost billions through compromised Discord servers. When an admin account is hacked, attackers can post phishing links to announcement channels that reach thousands of users instantly.

  • A single compromised admin can drain an entire community
  • Traditional moderation bots detect threats AFTER posting
  • No cryptographic proof of who authorized announcements
  • Protection ends when users leave Discord

The Protection Gap

Current security solutions protect Discord servers but not users. The actual wallet drain happens in the browser‚Äîafter users click and leave Discord.
