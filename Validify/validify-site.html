FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Will Validify ever ask for my seed phrase?
NEVER. Validify only uses wallet signature verification through MetaMask. We never ask for, store, or have access to your private keys or seed phrases. If anyone claiming to be from Validify asks for this information, it's a scam.
How does multi-signature verification work?
When you want to post a link, use /validify post-link. The bot DMs all registered admins with unique approval codes. Each admin has 30-60 seconds to approve. Only when your threshold (e.g., 3 of 5 admins) is met does the link get posted. Everything is logged on-chain.
What permissions does the bot need?
We follow a minimum-permissions approach: View Audit Log, View Channels, Read Message History (only in enabled channels), and Send Messages (for alerts). We do NOT require Administrator permission for basic features.
What is VLFY token?
VLFY is an ERC-20 token used to pay for Validify subscriptions. Currently on Sepolia testnet (free to claim from our faucet). On mainnet, you'll be able to purchase VLFY with ETH on DEXs.
When will the Discord bot be available?
Currently in private beta (Q1 2025). Public beta launches Q2 2025, with full production release planned for Q3 2025. Join our waitlist to get early access.
Can I use Validify on multiple servers?
Basic and Advanced tiers support single-server protection. The Pro tier includes multi-server dashboard for managing security across all your communities from one place.
